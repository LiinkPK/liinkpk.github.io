About Me

● Altruistic developer and musician who builds tools for things I actually care about: emulation, preserving old games, usability, and scratching my own itch. Not a game dev, but I've been deep in the Switch and PPSSPP rabbit hole long enough to start contributing to it.

● Most of what I build is either solving a problem I ran into personally, or making life easier for the people around me. If the workflow is a mess, I fix it. If nobody made the tool I need, I make it myself.

● Part of the Switch Atmosphere/Switchway homebrew scene since 2020, helping people with CFW, hardware repairs, and everything between firmware and soldering chips in and out.

● Based in Barcelona, building things I actually use, trying to take it one project at a time but I always end up getting involved in more than two or three.
